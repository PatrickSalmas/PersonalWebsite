About Me

I grew up and spent most of my life in Manhattan Beach, California. I studied and graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science at the University of California San Diego where I focused my studies on artificial intelligence and web design. I have employed many of these concepts via personal projects, projects at UCSD, and doing web design with Three Wire Systems the past two summers. I now reside in Richmond, Virginia with my girlfriend Melissa Ly.

When I am not working on software, I enjoy exploring new things to do with friends, exercising, and watching sports. I enjoy playing ice hockey, as I have for the past twenty years, and chances are, you may find me at a local hockey rink on the ice from time to time.
